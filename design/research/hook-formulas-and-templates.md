### Common Opening Patterns Ranked by Mechanism

**Curiosity Gap Hooks** (strongest performer — 22% more likely to go viral):
- "The one thing most people get wrong about..."

- "Stop scrolling if you..." — filters audience instantly; those who match stay

**Story/Unfinished Narrative Hooks:**
- "I almost quit my job until I discovered this one thing..."
- "Story time: I got fired and it was the best thing..."

**Direct Address Hooks:**
- "Hey [niche] people, did you know..."
- Creates immediate belonging signal

## 4. Copywriting Frameworks Applied to Short-Form Video

### AIDA for TikTok/Reels

**A**ttention (0-3 sec) = Your hook. This is the only part that matters if nothing else lands.
**I**nterest (3-8 sec) = Expand with a specific claim, fact, or relatable scenario.
**D**esire (8-18 sec) = Show the transformation or outcome.
**A**ction (final 2-3 sec) = CTA.

Source: [Foreplay.co](https://www.foreplay.co/post/aida-copywriting-tiktok-ad)

### PAS (Problem-Agitate-Solution)

Dan Kennedy called PAS "the most reliable sales copywriting formula ever invented."

Best for: **Retargeting ads, pain-point content, limited copy space**

Video structure:
1. **Problem** (0-2 sec): "Tired of spending 3 hours editing background out of photos?"
2. **Agitate** (2-5 sec): "Every tool crashes, the edges look terrible, and you've wasted your whole afternoon"
3. **Solution** (5-15 sec): "This tool removes backgrounds in one click" + demo

Source: [CopyHackers](https://copyhackers.com/2015/10/copywriting-formula/), [Animus Studios](https://www.animusstudios.com/blog/ad-formats-and-structures-combining-copywriting-formulas-with-storytelling-frameworks)

### BAB (Before-After-Bridge)

Best for: **Transformation content, testimonials, product reveals**

Video structure:
1. **Before** (0-3 sec): Show the painful current state
2. **After** (3-8 sec): Show the transformed state
3. **Bridge** (8-15 sec): "Here's how I did it" / product reveal

### Strategic Matching

| Framework | Best For | When to Use |
|-----------|----------|-------------|
| AIDA | Cold traffic, awareness | New audience, broad targeting |
| PAS | Retargeting, pain-point content | Audience knows the problem exists |
| BAB | Transformation stories | Product/service with clear before/after |
| Hybrid (PAS hook + AIDA body) | High-performance ads | Advanced creative testing |

Source: [TikAdSuite](https://tikadsuite.com/blog/tiktok-ad-copywriting-formulas/)

## 7. Call-to-Action Formulas: What Drives Saves, Shares, Comments

### CTA Performance Ranking

| CTA Type | Relative Performance |
|----------|---------------------|
| "Save this for later" | **Best performer** — outperforms "like if you agree" by 15% |
| "Tag someone who needs this" | Strong for shares |
| "Like if you agree" | Moderate |
| "Comment below" | **Underperforms** vs alternatives |

Source: [SamAutomation](https://samautomation.work/blog/tiktok-captions-engagement-strategy/)

### Aggregate CTA Impact

- Clear CTAs boost engagement by **23-31%** ([HubSpot via various](https://samautomation.work/blog/tiktok-captions-engagement-strategy/))
- Videos with CTAs boost click-through rates by **380%** and conversions by up to **80%** ([RecurPost](https://recurpost.com/blog/social-media-call-to-action-examples/))

### CTA Placement Strategy

**Mid-roll CTAs outperform end-frame CTAs** for comments — viewers are already invested when the prompt appears. Examples:
- Mid-roll: "Tag someone who'd relate"
- End-frame: "Which would you choose?"
- On-screen graphic: "Save this for later" overlay

### CTA-to-Content Matching

| Content Type | Best CTA |
|-------------|----------|
| How-to / tutorial | "Save this for later" |
| Relatable / emotional | "Share if you agree" / "Tag someone" |
| Controversial / opinion | "Comment your take" / "Do you agree?" |
| Before/after transformation | "Share this with someone who needs it" |
| List / tips | "Save this" + "Follow for part 2" |

Source: [Plann](https://www.plannthat.com/100-ctas-for-2020/), [Drive Editor](https://driveeditor.com/blog/best-cta-formats-for-short-videos)

## 8. Caption/Description Formulas That Boost Engagement

### Caption Length Sweet Spot (Instagram)

| Caption Length | Engagement Rate |
|---------------|----------------|
| 125-150 words | **3.1%** (optimal) |
| 50 words or fewer | 2.2% |
| 200+ words | 2.4% |

Source: [SamAutomation](https://samautomation.work/blog/tiktok-captions-engagement-strategy/)

### TikTok Caption Limits

As of 2025: **4,000 characters** (up from 2,200). More room for SEO keywords and storytelling.

### Power Words for Captions

"Secret," "Banned," "Exposed," "Finally," "Shocking" — create immediate intrigue in the first line.

### Caption Formulas That Work

**1. The Curiosity Gap:**
"I tried [X] for 30 days. Here's what happened..." (leaves resolution for the video)

**2. The Controversial Take:**
"Unpopular opinion: [bold claim]" (drives comments/debate)

**3. The Direct Value Promise:**
"3 things I wish I knew about [X] before I started" (drives saves)

**4. The POV Caption:**
"POV: You discover [specific transformation]"
- Example: "POV: You discover the 30-second ab exercise that changed everything" = 2.3M views, 340K likes

**5. The Question Caption:**
"What would you do if [scenario]?" (drives comments)

### Dual-Format Strategy

Creators who used **Reels for reach + feed posts for community** outperformed single-format strategies by **32%**.

Source: [Buffer State of Engagement 2026](https://buffer.com/resources/state-of-social-media-engagement-2026/)

### Reply to Comments

Accounts that reply to comments outperform those that don't by up to **42% on Threads** and **30% on LinkedIn** (Buffer, 52M posts analyzed).

## 9. Sound Selection Strategy

### Impact of Audio on Performance

| Factor | Data Point |
|--------|------------|
| Trending sound usage | **+58% engagement** vs no trending sound |
| Audio importance | **88% of TikTok users** say sound is key to their experience |
| Trending audio boost | **+48% reach** |
| Silent videos | **-19% engagement** |
| Too many overlapping sounds | Confuses viewers, hurts retention |
| Influencer sound adoption | Boosts sound usage by **up to 70%** |

Sources: [Shortimize](https://www.shortimize.com/blog/analyze-tiktok-video-how-your-audio-choices-affect-viewer-retention), [HAWX Tech](https://www.hawxtech.net/2026/03/the-ultimate-tiktok-ads-sound-strategy.html), [Buffer](https://buffer.com/resources/how-to-find-trending-tiktok-sounds/)

### Trending Sound Lifecycle

- TikTok trends typically last **5-7 days**
- **40% of TikTok users** discover new music through the Music Tab before hearing it anywhere else
- Early adoption (before peak) gives the biggest algorithmic boost
- The algorithm prioritizes **format-friendly audio** (skits, transitions, reveals)

### Sound Strategy Rules

1. **Match audio to content emotion** — misaligned audio/visuals disrupt retention
2. **Sync key moments to audio beats/lyrics** — increases perceived production quality
3. **Don't rely solely on trending sounds** — combine with quality hooks and retention strategies
4. **Original audio has value** for brand identity, but trending sounds win for discovery
5. **Voiceover in ads** leads to an additional **12% increase in conversion rate** ([TikTok for Business](https://ads.tiktok.com/business/en-US/blog/creative-best-practices-top-performing-ads))

### Optimal Number of Hashtags

| Platform | Optimal Count | Source |
|----------|--------------|--------|

| Instagram Posts | **5-15** balanced mix | [Sendible](https://www.sendible.com/insights/hashtags-for-instagram-reels) |

### Hashtag Impact on Reach

| Metric | Data |
|--------|------|
| Reels with hashtags vs without | **+43% reach, +47% engagement** |
| 3+ relevant hashtags on Reels | **+26% discovery** |
| 9-11 hashtags on Instagram posts | **+79.5% reach** vs fewer/none |

Source: [Cropink](https://cropink.com/instagram-hashtag-statistics), [Later](https://later.com/blog/how-many-hashtags-on-instagram/)

### Trending vs. Niche: The Mix Matters

**Trending hashtags** (#fyp, #viral): Broad reach, high competition, diluted targeting
**Niche hashtags** (#BookTok, #LatteArt): Smaller audience, higher engagement, better targeting

Best strategy: **Blend both.** Use trending for discovery, niche for community.

Notable niche example: **#BookTok** saw **99x+ growth** — niche communities can be massive.

Source: [Metricool](https://metricool.com/tiktok-study/), [Later](https://later.com/blog/ultimate-guide-to-using-instagram-hashtags/)

### Where Views Actually Come From

For accounts with fewer followers, **75%+ of impressions come from the "For You" page** — not from hashtag search or followers. Hashtags help the algorithm categorize your content, but the FYP is the real distribution engine.

Source: [Metricool 2024 TikTok Study](https://metricool.com/tiktok-study/) (87,600 accounts, 1M+ videos analyzed)

### Platform-Specific Reels Caveat

Unlike standard Instagram posts where niche hashtags work best, **Reels benefit more from timely/popular tags** that are actively searched.

Source: [Shortimize](https://www.shortimize.com/blog/best-reels-hashtags-for-instagram-to-go-viral)

## Appendix: The Hook Formula (3-Part Structure)

Source: [vidIQ](https://vidiq.com/blog/post/viral-video-hooks-youtube-shorts/)

Every strong hook has three simultaneous components:

1. **Visual hook** — what they see in frame 1 (face with emotion, unexpected image, text overlay)
2. **Text hook** — on-screen text, 5-10 words max ("You've been doing this wrong")
3. **Verbal hook** — what you say out loud with energy in the first second

Best-performing Shorts have **70-90% viewed-vs-swiped ratio**.

Testing protocol: **Pick 1 hook trigger, test 3 variations per week, measure Viewed vs Swiped Away.**
